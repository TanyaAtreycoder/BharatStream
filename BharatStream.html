About BharatStream

BharatStream is a prototype initiative under the Ministry of Earth Sciences to monitor ocean hazards, empower communities, and build a resilient coastal ecosystem. This platform enables real-time hazard reporting, volunteer engagement, and predictive awareness for safer oceans.
